# 02 Vision Document

## Vision

Ritzy Studio is a calm, premium AI design workspace that helps a Dubai residential interior designer move from client room photos to concept imagery, purchasable product selections, and client-ready presentation material.

The product should feel like a private interior studio rendered in software: visual, precise, restrained, and honest about uncertainty.

## Product Promise

Ritzy Studio shortens the path from "client sent room photos" to "designer has a beautiful direction and a practical shopping list."

The product does this by combining:

- room photo analysis
- style and design brief capture
- concept-first AI image generation
- iterative designer critique
- real UAE product catalog search
- product substitution and budget tuning
- final grounded render generation
- client-ready shopping list and presentation

## What Makes Ritzy Studio Different

Generic image generators can create attractive interiors but often invent unavailable furniture. Retail websites contain real products but do not understand a room, a client brief, or a design direction.

Ritzy Studio bridges the two:

- concept generation explores a design direction
- product grounding finds real purchasable substitutes
- final renders are generated after product selection
- shopping lists remain tied to real retailer data

## Target Experience

The designer should be able to sit at her desk, upload a room photo, describe the desired feel, and receive design directions without first hunting products.

The product should then support a professional refinement loop:

- "Make this more contemporary."
- "The chairs are too expensive."
- "Use warmer wood."
- "Find something from Home Centre or 2XL."
- "Keep the sofa, change the coffee table."
- "Show a client-ready version."

## Product Stages

### Stage 1: Concept Direction

The app creates visual design directions from the room photo and brief. Product accuracy is not required yet. The objective is to converge on style, palette, layout, and mood.

### Stage 2: Product Grounding

After a direction is approved, the app searches its indexed retailer catalog for real products that match the concept by category, style, color, material, budget, and dimensions when available.

### Stage 3: Substitution

The designer can request cheaper, smaller, warmer, premium, alternate-retailer, or alternate-style products. The app presents real product options and explains why they match.

### Stage 4: Final Grounded Render

The app generates final room renders using selected product images and the original room as references. The result is a realistic client-facing visualization, with explicit caveats that exact SKU rendering is best-effort.

### Stage 5: Client Package

The app exports or presents:

- final render images
- selected products
- retailer names
- prices in AED
- dimensions where available
- availability status where available
- product page URLs
- estimated total cost
- uncertainty notes

## Quality Bar

The product is acceptable only if:

- the visual experience follows the locked design system
- product lists contain real products
- price and stock timestamps are visible when relevant
- missing dimensions are not hidden
- the designer can override AI selections
- the final output is professional enough to show to a client after designer review

## Out Of Scope For MVP

- full procurement or checkout
- guaranteed exact SKU visual rendering
- guaranteed fit from photo-only measurements
- full nine-retailer coverage if not technically or legally safe
- multi-designer team management
- client collaboration portals
- mobile LiDAR capture
- automated construction drawings
