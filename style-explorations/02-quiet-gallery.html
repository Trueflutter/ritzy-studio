Slow rooms,
sourced in Dubai.

A gallery's hush, brought to a working tool. The room remains the subject; the interface steps aside. Cormorant Garamond holds the hand-drafted feel of an architect's notebook, while DM Sans keeps the captions and prices clear and modern. Generous breathing room, hairline rules, a single warm ochre when something matters.
